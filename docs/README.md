# Let's Meet

Schedule a meeting with the Let's Meet module, a smart tool for finding the best time for your team to meet. Create a poll with multiple dates and time slots, invite participants to vote, and identify the best option for everyone.

## Key Features:

- **Participants:** Invite users to vote on their preferred time slots, ensuring the best turnout for the meeting.
- **Results:** View aggregated votes directly on the wall entry to easily identify the most popular time slots.
- **Notifications:** Users are notified when invited, and the poll creator is alerted once all votes are in.
